# Product Definition

## Project Purpose

This project exists to celebrate and share Singaporean food culture through a fun, interactive personality test format. Singaporeans share a deep connection with their local cuisine—from hawker center staples to beloved zi char dishes—and this app provides an entertaining way for friends to bond over their shared food preferences while discovering which iconic Singaporean dish best represents their personality.

## Problems Solved

### Social Connection Gap
- Creates natural conversation starters among friends, families, and colleagues
- Bridges generational gaps by framing local food culture in a format appealing to Gen Z
- Provides shareable content that sparks engagement on social media

### Entertainment Void
- Offers a locally-relevant alternative to generic Western personality quizzes
- Combines nostalgia for traditional Singaporean fare with modern app experiences
- Delivers quick, digestible entertainment suitable for mobile consumption

### Cultural Preservation
- Keeps Singaporean food culture alive and relevant for younger generations
- Celebrates the diversity of Singapore's culinary landscape
- Educates players about dishes they may not have tried

## How It Works

1. **Quiz Flow**: Users answer 10 carefully crafted questions that probe personality traits, preferences, and behaviors
2. **Scoring Logic**: Each answer maps to personality archetypes tied to specific Singaporean dishes
3. **Result Page**: Users receive their dish personality with:
   - A description of why this dish matches their personality
   - Engaging visuals and formatting
   - Easy sharing capabilities
4. **Social Sharing**: Results can be shared on social platforms, driving organic discovery

## User Experience Goals

### Primary Goals
- **Engaging**: Questions feel fun and relatable, not clinical or boring
- **Accurate-Feeling**: Results resonate—users should feel "that's so me!"
- **Shareable**: Results are Instagram Stories-ready and conversation-worthy
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for phone screens with thumb-friendly interactions

### Secondary Goals
- **Fast Load Times**: Quick transitions between questions
- **Replayable**: Encourage friends to take the quiz multiple times
- **Local Authenticity**: Feel genuinely Singaporean, not a generic template
- **Visual Appeal**: Aesthetic design that reflects Singapore's vibrant food culture

## Success Metrics (To Be Defined)
- Time spent on quiz completion
- Social shares generated
- Repeat visits / replay rate
- User satisfaction feedback
